# Miscellaneous Supplementary Lectures

This folder contains supplementary theory lectures for **Elements of AIML** that do not belong to a single unit but support revision and deeper understanding.

## Reading Order

- `Lecture-01`: `PCA, LDA, and ICA` in one place, with a detailed comparison.
- `Lecture-02`: worked derivation of eigenvalues and eigenvectors from a small data sample.

## How to Use

- Read `Lecture-01` after `Unit-03/Lecture-03` if you want a cleaner comparison of the three methods.
- Read `Lecture-02` when you want to understand where the PCA eigenvectors come from mathematically.
- Treat both lectures as supplementary; they reinforce Unit III rather than replace it.
